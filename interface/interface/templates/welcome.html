Informed Consent Agreement for Participation in a Research Study

This study will take ~30 minutes to complete.


The purpose of this study is to determine how listeners perceive musical groupings. You will listen to a number of musical excerpts and indicate the beginnings of perceived groups. By participating, you are contributing to an effort to better understand how our perceptual and cognitive mechanisms work, which can be used in the development of new musical technologies. The experiment should last about 15 minutes. As long as the volume is kept to a reasonable level, there should be few risks in the experiment.

Participants will be identified only by a randomly-generated ID in the data. If you are participating in this study via Amazon Mechanical Turk, you MTurk ID will also be stored, which can be linked to personal information. MTurk ID’s will be stored on a password-protected computer and will not be published. Any publication or presentation of the data will not identify you.

Your participation in this research is voluntary. You may decide to stop participating in the research at any time, though the experiment must be completed to the best of your abilities in order to receive payment. The project investigators retain the right to cancel or postpone the experimental procedures at any time they see fit.
